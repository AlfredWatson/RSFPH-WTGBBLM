Copper IUDs kill or destroy sperm by releasing small amounts of copper into the uterus, while hormonal IUDs release low doses of progesterone to thicken cervical mucus and thin the lining of the uterus. IUDs are very effective in preventing pregnancy and can be left in place for several years. Barrier methods of contraception: These physical barriers prevent sperm from reaching the egg.
