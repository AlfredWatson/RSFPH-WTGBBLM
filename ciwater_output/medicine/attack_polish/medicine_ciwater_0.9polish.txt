Radiation therapy employs high-energy beams, like X-rays, to eliminate cancer cells. This can be utilized either in combination with chemotherapy or as a standalone treatment. Chemotherapy entails the use of medications to eliminate cancer cells. These medications can be administered orally or intravenously, and they may be utilized in combination with other treatments, such as surgery or radiation therapy. It's crucial to remember that each person's cancer or treatment plan is unique, and the optimal course of action depends on many factors. I suggest thoroughly discussing all treatment options, along with their potential risks and benefits, with your dad's healthcare team. Additionally, seeking a second opinion if desired is advisable.
